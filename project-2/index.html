A light rain is falling outside, leaving your hair and shoulders slightly damp.

As you push open the door, the hallway lamp glows with a warm yellow light

—you know he's waiting for you.

Right now, what do you most want him to do?

A. Hoping he'd dash over the moment you arrived, give you a big hug, then beam like sunshine saying, ‘Welcome home! You've had a rough day, haven't you?’ Before chattering away about all the amusing things that happened to him today, sweeping away your damp spirits in an instant. B. I hope he's timed your arrival perfectly, with honey water at just the right temperature and warm slippers ready. He'll take your bag and whisper, ‘Have a hot shower first, don't catch a chill.’ By the time you emerge, dinner will be neatly laid out on the table. C. I hope he's sitting on the sofa reading, glances up at the door opening, and gives you a gentle smile: ‘Back already? Was it cold out?’ Then he'll pat the spot beside him, inviting you to rest your head on his shoulder for a moment. He won't ask too many questions, just softly stroke your hair, letting you decide whether to speak. D. I wish he'd be curled up on the sofa gaming. Hearing the door open, he'd say without turning his head, ‘Back already? There's cake I bought in the fridge—help yourself.’ But when you emerge changed, you'd find he'd already set up the controller for you. On the screen would be that two-player game you mentioned wanting to try last time. He'd raise an eyebrow at you: ‘Fancy a go?’
